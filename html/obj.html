AIM/OBJECTIVE FOR HOSTEL LIFE



  • To provide a home for Indian students and to help new arrivals to become and adjusted to the new environment;

  • To offer the right atmosphere for study and interchange of thoughts and ideas;

  • To foster among students representing different communities, creeds, languages and cultures of
    India the spirit of fellowship, understanding, service to the Nation and the sense of unity amidst diversity;

  • To help Indian students understand, appreciate and imbibe what is best in the tradition and culture of
    Great Britain and at the same time to prove to be worthy ambassadors of India;

  • To promote goodwill between India and Great Britain and foster an international outlook.
